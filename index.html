Jonathan Gruber I am a research fellow at the National University of Singapore in the working group of Prof. Huanchen Bao. Before that, I was a PhD student at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne. My PhD advisor was Prof. Donna Testerman. In June 2023, I will start a postdoc position at the University of York, funded by a postdoc mobility grant from the Swiss National Science Foundation. email: jgruber at nus.edu.sg
Research My reseach interest is broadly in representation theory, and more specifically in the following topics: - representations of reductive algebraic groups and related topics; - tilting objects in highest weight categories; - homological methods in representation theory; - tensor categories, tensor ideals; - (diagrammatic) categorification; - Coxeter groups, Hecke algebras, Soergel bimodules.
