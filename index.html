Jonathan Gruber I am a postdoctoral researcher at Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen-Nuremberg, in the working group of Peter Fiebig. Previously, I was a postdoc at the University of York (working with Chris Bowman and Stephen Donkin) and a research fellow at the National University of Singapore (in the working group of Huanchen Bao). I completed my PhD at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne in 2022; my PhD advisor was Donna Testerman. email: jonathan.gruber at fau.de Here is a picture of me.
Research My reseach interest is broadly in representation theory, and more specifically in the following topics: - representations of reductive algebraic groups and related topics; - tilting objects in highest weight categories; - homological methods in representation theory; - tensor categories, tensor ideals; - (diagrammatic) categorification; - Coxeter groups, Hecke algebras, Soergel bimodules.
